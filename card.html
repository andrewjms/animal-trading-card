Shar Pei

This breed was almost made extinct when China became a Comunist contry and the government enacted strict and hefty taxes on all dogs. It was considered the 'rarest breed' in the 60's and 70's

  • Origins: Ancient China
  • Name: Chinese for, "sand-skin"
  • Height: 18-20 inches
  • Weight: 45-60 pounds

This medium-sized breed is best known for it's loose, folds of skin around it's head, neck and shoulders. They are considerd loyal and intelegent guard dogs. They must be socialized early or will consider everone, human and dog alike, as a posable threat to thier charge.
